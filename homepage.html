Houseplants - Characteristics & Care



Welcome to Houseplants - Characteristics and Care! Here, we'll explore everything you need to know about nurturing various types of indoor greenery. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, our guides will help you choose the perfect plants for you and care for them with confidence.

Discover the unique traits of different houseplants, from their light and watering needs to tips about their natural habitat. With our easy-to-follow advice, you'll transform your home into a green oasis in no time. Join us on this journey to bring the beauty of nature indoors.


How Our Website Works

Use the menu at the top of the screen to navigate between our five main pages, which each contain a listing of select houseplants from the named category. You will find pictures of each plant and information on their unique characteristics and how to care for them. And if you want to find out more, you may click on the image of any plant to visit its Wikipedia page.

Check out the right side of the menu to navigate to our plant chart. If you already know the type of care you would be able to provide for your plants (i.e., how much sunlight your apartment gets or your climate), the plant chart is a great tool to quickly find matching plants. You can click on any plant in the chart to visit its entry.

We hope that our website helps you find the perfect plants for your home!
